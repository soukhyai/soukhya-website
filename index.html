About SOUKHYA

SOUKHYA (pronounced Soak-yah) is in the Los Angeles area and has been singing/performing for over 23 years. In March of 2023, she performed the National Anthem for the NBA Warriors at the Chase Center with 20,000 fans in attendance. Previously, she also had the opportunity to sing in front of 20,000 people for the San Jose Sharks and officially became the First Indian American Woman to sing the National Anthem for the NHL. She grew up learning Indian Classical Music for 15 years and continued to pursue music (Bollywood & Western) throughout her life and on her social media platforms.

SOUKHYA has also performed for the NCAA Basketball games at Purdue University & for Adobe’s CEO, Shantanu Narayen. During the pandemic, she created a singing TikTok account where she has over 500K+ supporters! Recently, SOUKHYA acted in two full length movies, and has released a couple songs available on Spotify & Apple Music. Check out her new song "Hypnotized" ! SOUKHYA loves to connect with her audience and truly hopes to connect with other South Asian women through her music.
